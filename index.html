LoL Guide

LoL Guide is an Android application that gives you every information you need about the game.

The app is ready for production, all I need is a production API key.

Features

  • Summoner Info: You can view a summoner's match history, match statistics, most played champions, kda's with those champions and season statistics.
  • Weekly Free Champions: You can view the free champion rotation of the week.
  • Champions: You can view all champions, champion skins, their abilities, watch their ability videos, and see their recommended builds.
  • Discounts: You can view weekly champion and skin discounts.
  • Items: You can view Items and item hierachy.
  • News: You can read news about League of Legends.
  • Live Broadcasts: You can view the live broadcasts on twitch.
